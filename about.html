Khauf | COVID-19 Short Film

"KHAUF" is an evocative film that unravels the gripping story of Aditi, portrayed by Muskan Sharma, a reel-life character who finds herself facing a real-life crisis when she contracts COVID-19. In the midst of this formidable challenge, Aditi makes the heart-wrenching decision to hide her symptoms, feigning normalcy and disregarding the warning signs of the virus. This emotionally charged narrative takes viewers on a journey through the labyrinth of fear, uncertainty, and familial bonds. Aditi's choice to conceal her condition sets off a chain reaction, plunging her family into a state of panic and chaos as they grapple with the unseen threat that looms over their lives.

Iddat | Part 1

"Iddat" is a compelling cinematic journey that delves into the complexities of love, forgiveness, and acceptance against the backdrop of a heart-wrenching story. The film follows the poignant tale of a courageous young woman who defies societal norms by eloping and marrying a man from a different caste. However, her husband's choices soon unravel a chain of events that shatter her life, leaving her at a crossroads. As the story unfolds, "Iddat" raises profound questions about the human capacity for forgiveness and the enduring bonds of family. Will her family find it within their hearts to forgive her and embrace her once more? This emotionally charged narrative navigates the depths of human emotions, offering viewers a poignant reflection on the power of love and the resilience of the human spirit in the face of adversity.
